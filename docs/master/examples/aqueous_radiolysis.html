Aqueous radiolysis¶

examples/aqueous_radiolysis.py is an example of a rather large system of reactions.

                             [--xend XEND] [-d DOSERATE] [-N N] [--nt NT]
                             [--nstencil NSTENCIL] [--logy] [--logt] [--logx]
                             [--name NAME] [--num-jacobian] [-s SAVEFIG] [-v]
